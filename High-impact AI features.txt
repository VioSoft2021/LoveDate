High-impact AI features:

Smart profile writer
Turns rough user notes into high-quality bio/prompts in their tone.

AI photo quality coach
Scores profile photos (clarity, framing, vibe) and suggests better primary photo.

Conversation sentiment + pacing assistant
Detects when chat is cooling down and suggests recovery prompts.

Date planner AI
Generates personalized first-date ideas from interests, location, time, budget.

Safety AI triage
Auto-prioritizes reports (harassment/scam risk) and summarizes evidence for admin.

Match explainability engine
Shows deeper “why this match” breakdown with dynamic tips to improve compatibility.

Re-engagement AI
Creates personalized nudges for inactive matches (“best next message” per thread).

AI moderation for circles
Flags toxic/spam posts and suggests soft/hard moderation actions.

Profile-to-profile question generator
Creates unique, context-aware icebreakers between two specific users.

Compatibility forecast
Predicts likely friction points and gives proactive communication advice.
