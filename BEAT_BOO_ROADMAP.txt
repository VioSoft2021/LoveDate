LOVEDATE - BEAT BOO ROADMAP (v1)
Saved: 2026-04-08

Goal
Build a personality-first dating + community app that can outperform Boo for your target audience.

Phase 1 - Personality Core (Now)
1. Upgrade matching engine with weighted personality signals, intent alignment, distance, shared interests, and trust signals.
2. Show "Why you match" explanations on Discover cards.
3. Show detailed compatibility breakdown in Full Profile.
4. Keep scoring transparent and human-readable (not black box).

Phase 2 - Community Layer
1. Interest Groups/Circles (Design, Travel, Coffee, etc.).
2. Group discovery and join/leave flows.
3. Group feed + pinned topics + lightweight moderation tools.
4. Group events (online and local meetups).

Phase 3 - Social Graph
1. Follow/friend model beyond romantic matches.
2. Graph recommendations from shared interests + group overlap.
3. "People like you" and "Friends of your circles" rails.
4. Relationship pathways: Date / Friends / Community.

Phase 4 - Trust and Safety Leadership
1. Dedicated moderation center (already started).
2. Report evidence attachments + admin notes + decision audit trail.
3. Escalation actions: warning, temporary restrict, block, ban.
4. Safety reputation score and repeat-offender detection.

Phase 5 - Retention and Quality
1. Daily prompts and community challenges.
2. Better onboarding and profile completion guidance.
3. Real-time chat reliability and sync across devices.
4. Push/email lifecycle notifications and re-engagement loops.

Phase 6 - Scale and Distribution
1. KPI dashboard: signup -> profile complete -> match -> chat reply -> day-7 retention.
2. Feature flags + staged rollouts + rollback checkpoints.
3. Mobile performance hardening.
4. Compliance baseline (privacy, terms, consent, account deletion/export).

Execution order
1) Personality Core
2) Community Layer
3) Social Graph
4) Trust and Safety
5) Retention and Quality
6) Scale and Distribution

Definition of success
- Users understand why they match.
- Community activity grows without lowering safety.
- Retention improves week over week.
- Moderation decisions are fast, clear, and auditable.
